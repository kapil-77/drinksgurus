Meet the Gurus

Inder mani Dubey (Sonu)

Expert Mixologist at Drink Guru, Dubai

Indermani, fondly known as Sonu, is a talented mixologist and experienced bartender based in Dubai. With a deep passion for the craft and years of experience in luxury events and high-end hospitality, Sonu brings creativity, precision, and a personal touch to every cocktail he serves. Known for his innovative blends, smooth technique, and warm hospitality, he transforms ordinary drinks into extraordinary experiences. At Drink Guru, Sonu is a trusted name behind the bar — crafting unforgettable moments, one glass at a time.

Praveen Sharma (Pranky)

The Master Mixologist at Drink Guru

Praveen is a seasoned mixologist and expert bartender with over a decade of hands-on experience behind some of the most vibrant bars in the industry. Known for his flair, precision, and creativity, he brings a unique blend of classic techniques and modern mixology trends to every glass he crafts. As a proud face of Drink Guru, Praveen is passionate about creating memorable experiences through tailor-made cocktails, organic ingredients, and flawless service. Whether it’s a private event or a luxury gathering, Praveen ensures every sip tells a story.
